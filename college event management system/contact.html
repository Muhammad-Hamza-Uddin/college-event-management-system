Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I register for events on CampusConnect? ‚ñº

Simply browse our event calendar, click on any event that interests you, and hit the "Register" button. You'll need to create a free account if you haven't already. Registration confirmations are sent via email.

Can student organizations create their own events? ‚ñº

Yes! Registered student organizations can create and manage their own events through our platform. Contact our team for organization verification and access to event creation tools.

Is CampusConnect free to use? ‚ñº

Yes, CampusConnect is completely free for all students, faculty, and staff. Our platform is funded by the university to enhance campus life and student engagement.

What if I need to cancel my event registration? ‚ñº

You can cancel your registration anytime before the event through your account dashboard. For events with limited capacity, this helps other students join the waitlist.

How do I get technical support? ‚ñº

You can reach our technical support team via email at support@campusconnect.edu, use the contact form above, or visit us during office hours in the Student Center Building.
