Our Story

How we started and where we're heading

From Idea to Reality

CampusConnect was born from a simple observation: college students were missing out on amazing campus events simply because they didn't know they existed. Our founders, a group of computer science students, experienced this firsthand when they discovered they had missed several networking events, cultural festivals, and academic workshops.

What started as a small project to help their own university has grown into a comprehensive platform serving thousands of students across multiple institutions. We believe that every student deserves to have access to the full spectrum of their campus experience.

Today, CampusConnect is more than just an event platform ‚Äì we're building communities, fostering connections, and helping students make the most of their college years.
